<p><p>The Fecal Pancreatic Elastase Testing market comprises two primary types: Fecal Pancreatic Elastase ELISA Kits and Rapid Fecal Pancreatic Elastase Kits. ELISA kits are laboratory-based assays that use enzyme-linked immunosorbent technology for the quantitative measurement of elastase in stool samples, offering high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, Rapid Fecal Pancreatic Elastase Kits provide quick results, typically used in point-of-care settings, enabling faster diagnosis of pancreatic insufficiency with simplified procedures, which enhances accessibility for both healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
