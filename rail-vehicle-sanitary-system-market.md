<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rail Vehicle Sanitary System market includes two primary types: vacuum toilet systems and pressured water flushing toilet systems. Vacuum toilet systems utilize suction to efficiently remove waste with minimal water, making them ideal for trains where space and water conservation are crucial. In contrast, pressured water flushing toilet systems use pressurized water to flush waste away, providing a more conventional sanitation approach. Both systems prioritize hygiene and comfort, catering to the specific needs of rail vehicles and enhancing passenger experience.</p></p>
