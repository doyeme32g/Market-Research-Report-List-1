<p><p>The structural glass market is segmented into frame type and frameless type. Frame type involves glass panels supported by metal or other material frames, often enhancing structural stability and design versatility. This option is commonly used in commercial buildings and showcases due to its strength. In contrast, frameless type features glass panels without visible supports, promoting a sleek, modern aesthetic. This category is popular in residential spaces and high-end applications, offering unobstructed views and a minimalist design.</p></p>
