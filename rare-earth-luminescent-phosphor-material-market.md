<p><p>The Rare Earth Luminescent Phosphor Material Market is categorized into several types based on luminescent mechanisms. Photoluminescence involves the absorption of light to emit visible radiation, commonly used in displays. Electroluminescence occurs when materials emit light in response to an electric current, prevalent in solid-state lighting. Radiation luminescence refers to light emission as a response to ionizing radiation, used in safety and monitoring applications. Chemiluminescence involves light produced through chemical reactions, often seen in glow sticks. Other types may include thermoluminescence and bioluminescence applications.</p></p>
