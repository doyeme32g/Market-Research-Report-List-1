Organic coatings, often based on polymers, provide flexibility and chemical resistance, enhancing battery performance. Inorganic coatings, typically derived from minerals, offer superior thermal stability and ion conductivity. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing lithium battery efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
