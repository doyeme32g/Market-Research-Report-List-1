<p><strong>The Photocured Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type Photoinitiator</li><li>Cationic Type Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocured materials market includes various types of photoinitiators, primarily categorized into free-radical and cationic types. Free-radical photoinitiators generate free radicals upon exposure to light, triggering polymerization in applications like inks and coatings. They are widely used due to their efficiency and speed. Cationic photoinitiators, on the other hand, generate cations, initiating a different polymerization process suitable for more specialized applications, such as in certain adhesives and high-performance coatings. Each type serves specific industry needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
