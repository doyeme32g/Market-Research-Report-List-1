<p><p>The polyurethane wheels market is segmented primarily into two types: PPG (Polypropylene Glycol) polyurethane wheels and PTMEG (PolyTetramethylene Ether Glycol) polyurethane wheels. PPG polyurethane wheels are known for their durability and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, PTMEG polyurethane wheels offer enhanced flexibility and resilience, providing superior performance in environments requiring shock absorption and wear resistance. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, material handling, and recreational products.</p></p>
